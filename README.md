## Overview

CraftConnect is a discovery and networking platform designed for independent brands, artisans, and makers of handmade or DIY products who often struggle to gain recognition beyond large marketplaces like Amazon or Etsy.

Unlike traditional e-commerce platforms, **CraftConnect is not an online marketplace**. Instead, it serves as a **discovery and collaboration platform** where customers can explore unique brands, brand owners can establish a professional online presence, and content creators can discover collaboration opportunities that align with their audience and niche.

The platform focuses on building meaningful connections through AI-powered discovery, personalized recommendations, and seamless communication between all three user roles.

## Core User Roles

### Customer

- Discover handmade brands
- Browse products
- Save favorite brands
- Send custom requests
- Message brands
- Rate brands
- Receive personalized recommendations

### Brand Owner

- Manage brand profile
- Manage product catalog
- Collaborate with creators
- Message customers and creators
- Access AI Studio
  - Brand Story Generator
  - Product Description Generator
  - Creator Match AI

### Creator

- Build a professional portfolio
- Discover brands
- Pitch collaboration proposals
- Receive AI-powered brand recommendations
- Communicate with brands
